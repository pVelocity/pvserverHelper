### ``createSalesforceProviderModel(jsapi, dataSetId, username, password)``
Creates provider model with ``username``, ``password`` on ``dataSetId``.
- `jsapi` `<Object>`
- `dataSetId` `<String>`
- `username` `<String>`
- `password` `<String>`

```js
pvh.createSalesforceProviderModel(jsapi, 'Salesforce', 'person@person.com', 'abc123');
```
