### ``createSalesforceProviderModel(jsapi, access_token, instance_url)``
Creates provider model with ``access_token`` on ``instance_url``.
- `jsapi` `<Object>`
- `access_token` `<String>`
- `instance_url` `<String>`

```js
pvh.createSalesforceProviderModel(jsapi, 'f13fafasfas', 'http://login.salesforce.com');
```
